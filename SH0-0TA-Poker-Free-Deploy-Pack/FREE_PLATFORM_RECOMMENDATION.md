# Recommended Free Platform

## Best $0 MVP stack

Use:

1. **Render Free** for backend and frontend hosting.
2. **Neon Free** for PostgreSQL persistence.
3. **Stripe Checkout** for cosmetic/VIP purchases only.
4. **Render default subdomain** for free HTTPS, so you do not need to buy a domain yet.

## Why not Vercel Hobby for this app?

Vercel Hobby is great for personal projects, but your app includes purchases/revenue. Vercel documentation and pricing guides describe the Hobby plan as personal/non-commercial. So for a revenue-generating app, do not rely on Vercel Hobby as your primary free commercial host.

## Free-tier warning

A free app may sleep, cold-start, pause, or hit usage limits. This is fine for MVP testing, but upgrade once real users arrive.
